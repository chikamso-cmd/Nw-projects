AUTHENTIC ITALIAN RESTAURANT IN HANDFORTH

DE KRISTEIN'S VERDE LOUNGE

Come join us every Friday & Saturday and have a taste of Handforth’s best cocktails, in De kristein'sVerde lounge!

We pride ourselves on the quality of our food. Each dish is carefully crafted by our Sicilian chef who brings over 20 years’ experience to the table. We use high quality, fresh ingredients in all of our dishes, including seasonal produce to ensure our customers have the very best dining experience.

At De kristein's restaurant, we like to keep life interesting with exciting events, bespoke menus and promotions every month.

De kristein's provides Live Music for every last Friday of each month, so come on down to experience the best authentic Italian food and the best live music in Hadforth! If you’re looking for a delicious Sunday dinner with a difference, visit us from 12pm – 6pm and kids eat free with every adult meal purchased.

You can stay up-to-date and join in the conversation by following us on twitter and liking us on Facebook. Or join our email list and we’ll send you exclusive special offers and keep you updated throughout the year.
